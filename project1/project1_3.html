Webtoons are Digital Free Comics Service Platform, a type of webcomic that originated in South Korea. While webtoons were mostly unknown outside of the country during their inception, there has been a surge in popularity internationally thanks in great part to most comics being read on smartphones. As digital comics have emerged as a popular medium, the print publication of manhwa in South Korea has decreased.
Usually, each webtoon publishes the next episode once a week. The webtoon that bring its episode every Monday, I call it Monday's webtoon. And, same methods for the other days. I pick seven different webtoons for the seven days and introduce them.
My name is Seoju Jung majored in Graphic design. In this website's I introduce and document my own experience in the form of an online zine. I have watched webtoons since I was in elementary school. And I picked 7 favorite different webtoons to share my experiences.
