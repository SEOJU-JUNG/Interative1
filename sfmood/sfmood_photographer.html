Hi, I am from South Korea and currently attending CCA, California College of the Arts, which is located in the San Francisco Bay area. Whenever I go out and have fun in this city, I've taken photos of some beautiful places here. I really like to use some warm and moody filters when taking photos. I put the address of the locations for each photo so you may visit there and see them in person. I hope you enjoy my photos.
